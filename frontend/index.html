Neural Connectome Bit Rate Game

A connectome is a complete wiring diagram of the brain, mapping every neuron and every synaptic connection between them.

Your objective is to reconstruct as much of the connectome as possible in 60 seconds. Each round, a neuron fires and propagates a signal to the next. Identify which neuron fired and classify the type of synapse it projects.

Excitatory (E) — a bright green synapse activates its target.
Inhibitory (I) — a bright red synapse suppresses its target.

Press E (excitatory) or R (inhibitory) to set the synapse type, then click the firing neuron in the scene.

Ex. neuron G pulses green → press E and click neuron G
